HVAC System

The services include Heat Load Calculation, selection of equipment, Design and Energy Conservation in terms of savings in power, building automation (BMS), etc. AC system consists of DX Type, Chilled Water system viz: Reciprocation Screw Chillers & amp; Centrifugal Chillers in the range of 100-3500 TR central plants, Chilled Water Pipes, fittings, valves and related accessories, etc. Fabrication of ducts in accordance with SMACNA standard and to install as per ASHRAE standard. Specialized systems viz: Smoke Management Systems, Staircase Pressurization system, 24x7 Monitoring system, etc. The installation team has been trained to take care of specific noise /vibration control measures prior to erection of any equipment viz: Chillers, Pumps, Air Handing Units, Fan Coil Units, Mechanical Ventilation, etc. Air balancing / water balancing prior to final testing and commissioning activities in accordance with CIBSE

  • Chilled Water system
  • Piping Networks
  • HVAC Equipment.
  • Ventilation Fans
  • Exhaust & Pressurization
  • Ducting & Accessories
  • Noise/Vibration Control
  • Air/Water Balancing
